Smoky Sausage Hotpot

Description

A classic combination with chicken and creamy sauce, served atop piping hot pasta – it’s all the comfort both you and this underrated herb were looking for.

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 bunch of tarragon (or 1tsp if using dried tarragon)
  • 2 zucchinis
  • 1kg chicken thigh
  • 2tsp chicken stock dissolved in 4tsp hot water
  • 400g rigatoni
  • 600g thickene cream
  • 4tsp plain flour

Steps

  1. Bring a large saucepan of salted water to the boil.
  2. Chop the zucchini into 2cm chunks.
  3. Chop the chicken into 2cm chunks.
  4. Pick and finely chop the tarragon leaves.
  5. Combine the chicken stock and hot water.
  6. Add the rigatoni to the boiling water and gook for 7-8 minutes. Drain, return to the saucepan and drizzle with olive oil to prevent sticking.
  7. While the pasta is cooking, heat a large frypan over a medium-high heat. Add the zucchini (no olive oil) and cook for 5-6 mintues, or until lightly golden and charred. Transfer to a bowl and season with a pinch of salt and peppa.
  8. Return the frying pan to a medium-high heat with a drizzle of olive oil. Add the chicken and cook for 4-5 mins or until browned and cooked through.
  9. Add the garlic, and cook for 1 minute.
  10. Pour in the cream and chicken stock, mix well, and reduce the heat to medium to simmer for 5 mins.
  11. Stir in the tarragon and zucchini, then add the pasta and stir through to coat in the sauce.
  12. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
